# YTerMusic

- [https://github.com/ccgauche/ytermusic](https://github.com/ccgauche/ytermusic)

## Keybinds

|key              |action                           |
|:-               |:-                               |
|space            |play/pause                       |
|enter            |select                           |
|f                |search                           |
|s                |shuffle                          |
|r                |remove song from current playlist|
|right/>          |skip 5 sec forward               |
|left/<           |skip 5 sec back                  |
|ctrl+right/ctrl+>|next song                        |
|ctrl+left/ctrl+< |prev song                        |
|+                |volume up                        |
|-                |volume down                      |
|down             |down                             |
|up               |up                               |
|esc              |exit current menu                |
|ctrl+c/ctrl+d    |exit                             |
